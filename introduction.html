About Us

Introduction

Welcome to Collinz

Collinz Education Group was founded in the year 2010, initially conceived as a tutorial programme and in the later years, developed to a full time learning centre with intention of guiding students as they undergo the International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE).

We currently have four centres that offer tutorial programmes - Collinz Tutoring & Learning Academy (Subang Jaya), Botanic IGCSE Tutorial Centre (Klang), Collinz Uptown Tutorial Centre (Damansara), and Collinz Sri Hartamas Tutorial Centre (Sri Hartamas).

Furthermore, we have two full-time learning centres – Collinz International located at Damansara Uptown and Sri Botanic IGCSE Learning Centre located at Bandar Botanic, Klang.

At Collinz, we not only believe in academic excellence, but also in instilling the right values. Our learning process includes student-teacher interaction, emphasizing on critical thinking and developing students to become confident, independent individuals.

Our Core Values


H. ierarchy of learning - To educate students using methods that develops critical thinking, exploring ideas, and embracing challenges.

E. thics - To instill the good values into each student to grow as an individual that constantly cares for the society and the environment.

L. eadership - To develop students’ leadership skills into becoming a responsible and confident individuals that motivate and inspire others.

P. hysical development - To cultivate the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle and help students discover their talents.
