Tutorial Programmes

This tutorial programme is available for students of the Cambridge syllabus from Year 1 to 11, IGCSE, A-Level, Pre-U, IB and Foundation Programmes. Each tutorial group has its student-teacher ratio kept at an ideal number to ensure students receive their needed attention and develop their full potential. At Collinz Tutorial, we have qualified and experienced tutors ready to guide and help students to achieve excellent results.

• Small class size factor, impressively low student-teacher ratio.
• Qualified & experienced tutors with proven track record in Cambridge syllabus.
• A total of over 600 students from international schools in Klang Valley.
