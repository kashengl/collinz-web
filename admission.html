Admission

Admission Details

How to apply

Admission for both tutorial and full time learning are available throughout the year. Below are the step by step guide for application process.
For further inquiries, do contact us and we’ll be happy to explain in depth!



Full Time Academic
Program (IGCSE)

Step 1 | Application
Parents are to drop by at our Full Time Learning Centre to fill up the enquiry form and provide academic records from applicant's previous school and necessary documents.

Step 2 | Assessment
After submitting the application form, every new applicant is required to sit for an assessment test to determine which year group he/she will be placed.

Step 3 | Outcome of the assessment
Applicant and parents will be called for a meet-up session to discuss on the outcome of the assessment test and confirm on the year group placement.

Step 4 | Admission
If application is successful, applicants will be offered a place to study at our learning centre. An offer letter will be issued together with other necessary forms for documentation.


Tutorial Classes

Step 1 | Inquire
Parents to inquire informations about the subjects and time slots.

Step 2 | Application
Parents are to drop by at our Tutorial Centre to fill up the application form and make payment.
