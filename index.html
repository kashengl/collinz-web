Full Time IGCSE Programs

At Collinz, we not only believe in delivering academic excellence, but also in instilling the right values. Our learning process includes student-teacher interaction, emtphasizing on critical thinking and developing students to become confident, independent individuals.

  • Qualified Tutors

  • Small Class Size

  • Recognitions

Qualified Tutors

Our team of teachers are highly qualified and experienced with proven track record in teaching the Cambridge syllabus (KS1, KS2, KS3, IGCSE and A-Level/IB) as well as Foundation Program.

Small Class Size

Class size is one factor to consider when evaluating learning effectiveness. Smaller classes achieve better outcomes. A majority of our tutorial classes have an impressively low student-teacher ratio.

Recognition

We are established and well-known for our tutorial programmes. We have a many students from various well established international schools in Klang Valley doing our tutorial programmes.

Scholastic Guidance

This tutorial programme is available for students of the Cambridge (CIE) syllabus from Year 1 to 11, IGCSE, A-Level, Pre-U, IB and Foundation Programme. Each tutorial group’s student-teacher ratio is kept at an ideal number to ensure that students receive individual attention and develop their full potential. At Collinz Tutorial, we have qualified and experienced tutors ready to guide and help students to achieve excellent results.
