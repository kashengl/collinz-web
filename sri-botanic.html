Sri Botanic

Full Time Academic Program | 9am-3.10pm


Sri Botanic is located at the major residential area, Bandar Botanic, Klang. Within a few minute drive from Jusco, Bukit Tinggi / Wholesale City GM Klang. Our centre is easily accessible from KESAS highway. The three-storey building can accommodate up to 300 students. (Full address: No. 46-1, Jln Kasuarina 3/KS 07, Bandar Botanic 41200, Klang.)

Our academic programme offers The Cambridge International Examination (CIE) Curriculum. Our school hours are from 9am to 3.10pm. It is available for students from Year 7 to 11 (IGCSE). Alternatively, we provide admission for Pre- Year 7 classes for younger students who are diligent and self-motivated. At Sri Botanic, we want to create a holistic experience and an interactive environment, engaging students to learn and nurturing them to achieve more than what they are capable of. Our class sizes are small, ensuring each student is given the amount of attention needed. With the support of our highly qualified and experienced teachers, we aim to maximize the unique potential of each student, challenging them to go beyond their limitations.

Our focus on student involvement does not end with the classroom, as we believe extra-curricular activities is as important as academic excellence. We provide extra-curricular activities to encourage students to be sociable, to discover their talents, as well as building team spirit and leadership skills. With this, we aim to create a fun and enjoyable learning environment.
