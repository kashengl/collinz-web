Career

At Collinz Education Group, we’re always in search for inspiring
individuals who have the passion in working with children and
adolescences. Individuals who are highly motivated in improving
the education industry are more than welcome too!

Educator

We are currently accepting applications for tutor/teacher positions. Individuals who are experienced, dedicated and passionate are encouraged to apply. Full time or part time positions available.


Qualifications:

- Minimum 2 years teaching experience in any related field (School Teachers, Lecturers, Professors, PhD Holders, and Examiners etc.)
- Holds at least a Bachelor's Degree
- Passionate in building teaching as a long term career
- Familiar with Cambridge International Examination (CIE) syllabus and format.
- Newly qualified teachers are welcome to apply.



Marketing and Management Team

We are currently accepting applications for marketing and management position. Individuals who have high-degrees of self-motivation and a positive mindset are encouraged to apply. Full time position available.

Qualifications:

- Minimum 2 years working experience in any related field.
- Holds at least a Degree/Diploma
- Good communication skills, both in written and spoken English. Mandarin and cantonese will be an added advantage.
